Who We Are

BUP Info Tech Club is a student-led technology community at Bangladesh University of Professionals. The club brings together students who are passionate about programming, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, data science, and emerging technologies.

Through workshops, competitions, seminars, and collaborative projects, the club creates opportunities for members to learn practical skills and stay connected with the rapidly evolving tech industry.

Our Vision

To create a vibrant technology community at BUP where students explore innovation, develop technical expertise, and contribute to solving real-world problems.

Our Mission

  • • Promote learning through hands-on workshops and hackathons.
  • • Encourage collaboration among students with shared technical interests.
  • • Connect members with industry professionals and opportunities.
  • • Build a strong alumni network within the technology sector.

What We Do

Workshops

Hands-on technical workshops covering programming, cybersecurity, web development, and other modern technologies.

Competitions

Hackathons, programming contests, and tech competitions that challenge members to solve real problems.

Networking

Events connecting students with alumni, industry professionals, and technology leaders.
